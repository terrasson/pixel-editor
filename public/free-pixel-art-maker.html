Frequently asked questions

Is this pixel art maker really free?

Yes, completely free. No credit card, no free trial, no premium tier needed for core features. The full editor is available to everyone at no cost.

Are there watermarks on exported pixel art?

No watermarks. When you export your pixel art as PNG or GIF, the file is clean — no branding, no watermarks, yours to use as you like.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required to draw and export. You only need a free account if you want to save projects to the cloud and access them from multiple devices.

Can I use my pixel art commercially?

Yes. The pixel art you create belongs entirely to you. You can use it in games, apps, social media, merchandise, or any commercial project without fees or attribution.

Will it always be free?

Pixel Editor is a personal open source project. The goal is to keep it free for everyone. The current features will always remain free.
