How to use Pixel Editor online

Getting started takes less than 30 seconds. Here's how it works:

1

Open the editor

Click "Open Editor" — no account or download needed. The 32×32 canvas loads instantly in your browser.

2

Pick a color and draw

Select a color from the palette or enter a hex code. Tap or click pixels to fill them. Use the eraser to correct mistakes.

3

Add frames for animation (optional)

Click the + button to add frames and create frame-by-frame animations. Use onion skinning to see the previous frame while drawing.

4

Export or share

Export as PNG or animated GIF, or share via a direct link. Publish to the community gallery to get feedback.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to download anything to use this pixel art editor?

No. Pixel Editor runs entirely in your browser. There is nothing to download or install — just open the site and start drawing immediately.

Does the online pixel art editor work on mobile?

Yes. Pixel Editor is fully optimized for touch screens. It works on iPhone, iPad, Android phones and tablets, as well as any desktop browser.

Can I save my pixel art online?

Yes. Create a free account to save your projects to the cloud and access them from any device. Without an account, your work is saved locally in your browser.

What file formats can I export?

You can export your pixel art as PNG images or as animated GIFs if you created multiple frames. Both formats are supported natively.

Is Pixel Editor free?

Yes, completely free. No subscription, no credit card, no watermarks on your exports. The full editor is available to everyone at no cost.
